Joining Rackonnect -

What is rackonnect?

Rackonnect is a social networking app for connecting with potential racket players around you. Our key features help you connect with fellow racket players looking for a buddy! You can also compete for the leaderboard and conquer the charts by participating and, or organizing racket tournaments through Rackonnect.

Is the registration free?

Yes, registration at Rackonnect is absolutely free of cost. You can either login via Facebook/Twitter OR login via your mobile phone number.

Choose your sport

Update Profile > Choose Sport. You can choose single or multiple racket sports you wish to play. You can select more sports after your profile is created by going on ‘Edit Profile’

Choose your level

Choose sport > choose level. You need to assess your ability by yourself and select the level that best suits you.

 

Find your Rackmates -

Filters

Homepage >select filter icon > Filter Page and pick the filters basis your convenience. These can be changed using the same steps at any given time

Chat

Homepage > Chat Now. You can chat with any player you deem best suited as your opponent. Once the match is over and the scores are filled in, the chat gets deleted automatically

Create Match

Homepage > Chat Now > Create Match. This will help you fix a match with suitable racket players around you by filling basic details such as match name, location, and time.

 

Scoring & Results -

Two way validation

Match end > start scoring > select game type > put match score (not game score) > save score. Once fed, the score goes to the other person (singles) or the other persons (doubles). The scores will get updated once it is accepted by the second person.

30minutes acceptance

Once the strong by the host, the other player has to validate it within 30 minutes or else the scores will not get updated and both the players match wouldn’t get counted in the system.

Wrong scoring

If the host has put the wrong score then you can decline it and report the player.

LeaderBoard –

Once the scores are updated, the win % is calculated and fed in the leaderboard. Leaderboard works on the basis of games played and won.

Points -

  • Download App – 100 points
  • Refer Friends – 50 points
  • Referring Vouchers/Code  – 50 points
  • Win – 50 Points
  • Loss – 10 points
  • DNS – (-100 points)

 

Tournament-

How to create?

Create match or tournament > tournament > basic details > game format > number of players > registration fee per player > create tournament. This helps you create your own tournament and make a name in the community. Pre determined algorithm; Prize Money – 40% to winner, 20% runners up, 25% to organizer

How to join?

Explore tournament > send request to join tournament > once joined you will be on the group chat.

Payment/Tournament Registration

Pay fee > join tournament

If total players are not complete.

The host needs a minimum of 50% of the total players to go ahead with the tournament. However, it is best suited if all players join the tournament.

Cancellation & Refunds

If the host cancels the tournament all players get refunded. Once joined the registration fee is non refundable until and unless the tournament is cancelled by the host.
